About - Smart AgrotTech System

Goal of smart agriculture with the advent of IOT is to provide the latest technology in agriculture and farming for quality production. The smart agriculture research is to ground a decision making support system for farm management. A system that optimizes and examines how high-tech farming can aid the production output as well as focuses on the preservation of resources. By providing them with the benefits of technological advancements, smart agriculture aims to reduce the heavy workload of the farm workers, hence improving their quality of life.
